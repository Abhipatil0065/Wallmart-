🛒 Walmart Data Analysis Dashboard
1. Business Problem

Retail businesses like Walmart handle massive transaction data across multiple locations, products, and time periods.

The key challenge is:

Identifying which products and regions drive profit
Tracking monthly performance trends
Comparing actual revenue vs targets
Detecting declining or underperforming areas quickly

Without a centralized dashboard, decision-making becomes slow and inefficient.

2. Goal of the Dashboard

The objective of this dashboard is to:

Provide a high-level overview of sales, profit, and transactions
Monitor month-over-month performance changes
Analyze geographical sales distribution
Compare actual revenue against targets
Enable stakeholders to make data-driven decisions quickly
3. Key Visuals Used in Dashboard
📊 KPI Cards
Total Transactions & Monthly Change (%)
Used to quickly track overall performance and trend direction
Helps identify growth or decline instantly
📋 Table (Product-wise Analysis)
Displays Product Brand, Profit, Profit Margin
Helps in identifying:
High-performing products
Low-margin products needing attention
🌍 Map Visualization
Shows Total Transactions by Country
Used for geographical analysis
Helps stakeholders understand market distribution globally
🧩 Treemap Chart
Displays Transactions by State and City
Best for hierarchical data visualization
Helps identify top-performing regions at a glance
📊 Bar Chart (Revenue vs Target)
Compares Actual Revenue vs Revenue Target
Helps in performance tracking against goals
🎯 Gauge Chart
Displays Revenue Target Achievement
Provides a quick visual of how close the business is to its goal
4. Business Impact

This dashboard helps businesses to:

📈 Improve decision-making speed with real-time insights
🎯 Track target achievement effectively
🌎 Identify high-performing regions and expansion opportunities
📉 Detect declining trends early
💰 Optimize product-level profitability
5. Key Insights Derived from Dashboard
🔻 Transactions decreased by ~3.5% compared to last month → indicates a short-term slowdown
💰 Total profit margin is around 0.60 (60%), showing stable profitability
🏆 Some product brands consistently generate higher profit margins, making them key revenue drivers
🌍 USA contributes the highest transaction volume, followed by other regions
🏙️ Cities like Los Angeles, Seattle, and Vancouver show strong performance
🎯 Revenue is close to target (~1.74M) but still has room for improvement
🚀 Conclusion

This dashboard provides a comprehensive retail performance view, enabling businesses to monitor KPIs, identify trends, and take strategic actions efficiently.# Wallmart
